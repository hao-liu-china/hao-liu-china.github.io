Brief Bio

I received the Ph.D. degree from Sun Yat-Sen University (SYSU) in 2023, supervised by Prof. Yulan Guo and the M.E. degree from National University of Defense Technology (NUDT) in 2018. Before that, I received the B.E. degree from University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC) in 2016. Currently, I'm a research fellow with the School of Computer Science and Engineering, Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore. My research interests focus on 3D deep learning and NeRF, particularly on 3D object detection and tracking.
